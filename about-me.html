Hey! if you didn't already know my name is Kelsi. I am originally from a small seaside town in Cornwall called Bude. Growing up living by the sea and being surrounded by nature constantly was such a privilege I will always be grateful for. It taught me to appreciate the small moments in life and take things at a slower pace.

Some of my favourite things to do in my spare time are: Sketching/Doodling, Spending time with family and friends, Playing video games (I am an absolute nerd when it comes to video games), Listening to my favourite music, Playing guitar; especially with my mates, Surfing and swimming, Helping my parents out around the farm, and spending time with my dog!

Fun Fact!

I used to want to be an Actor! I even studied acting at college for two years before I decided it wasn’t the path I wanted to go down.

The main productions I have been in, and the ones I’m most proud of, are Antigone by Sophocles and Replica by Titas Holder.

Acting really brought out a different side to me and helped me gain my confidence. It is also what introduced me into media! I was apart of a short film in my second year. The director showed me the process of post-production and I absolutely loved seeing how edited everything and created all the promotional content for the film and from that moment I started to consider doing a Media course in University if I didn't end up doing acting (which I didnt)
